What we do?

At heart we are a place for people with a common love and appreciation for Opera and classical music more broadly to share their passions. We are a social society. But we also provide a platform for young directors and producers to make new and exciting forms of Opera and singers a chance to perform. If you're interested in performing or producing an Opera please come to any of our socials or get into contact with us.

For people that want to put on their own production go to the Putting on a show section to find out more.

What do we stand for? (i.e. society constitution)

We want to share our passion and love for Opera and bring together like minded people. We want to make Opera accessible and introduce to new a different people. Please see our Constitution for more details...
